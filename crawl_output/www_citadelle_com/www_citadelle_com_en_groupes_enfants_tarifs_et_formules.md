# Admission fees and practical information
The rates shown on the page correspond to rates for the year 2025.
Rate for children under 4 and disabled children 
--- 
Free 
Group rates for children aged 4 and over (leisure center, school, student) and additional accompanying adult 
--- 
April to end of October | 4,70 € 
February to end of March and November to end of December | 3,70 € 
Children with disabilities | Free

Free admission request to be made to the Education Department of the City of Besançon + reservation request to be made to the Citadelle. | Admission for children paid by the City of Besançon and free for accompanying adults 
### Further information
 * Conditions for free child group escorts : >Schoolchildren : 1 free escort for every 10 children accompanied (1 free escort for every 5 children in nursery schools). >Leisure centers : 1 free escort for every 10 children accompanied. >Escort for disabled children : free.
 * If you wish, you can book guided tours or educational workshops, the cost of which is added to the admission price.Prices for tours and workshops: see directly Children's group offersFrom May to early July, one activity maximum per pupil.Admission to the Citadelle gives independent access to all the site's attractions: discovery of the site, museums and animal areas.
 * Children and students in groups must be accompanied by adults under whose supervision and responsibility they will remain for the duration of the visit and activity booked.

 * Secondary schools: possibility of using the Culture Pass Group
