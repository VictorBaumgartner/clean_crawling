What to see / What to do 
Vauban's masterpiece, a UNESCO World Heritage site, the Citadelle today houses three museums with the "Musées de France" label: the Musée comtois, the Muséum with its varied animal exhibits, and the Musée de la Résistance et de la Déportation.
