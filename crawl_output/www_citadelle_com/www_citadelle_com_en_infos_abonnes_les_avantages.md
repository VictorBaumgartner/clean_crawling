# The benefits
Enjoy unlimited access to the Citadelle!With the Cita'Pass, you can visit the Citadelle during the day as often as you like for 12 consecutive months* You'll benefit from a host of on-site advantages, as well as those offered by our partners.As an ambassador, you'll receive regular updates on the latest news from the Citadelle and its museums.
*from the date of purchase of your season ticket at the box office on your first visit. Your season ticket will be available within 15 days of purchase, and will be given to you on site on your first visit, after which time you must show proof of identity. If you buy your season ticket on site at the Citadelle and wish to visit the site on the same day, you will not pay the entrance fee due (for yourself).
### At the Citadelle :
 * Boutique de la Citadelle : 10% discount on souvenirs and 5% discount on books (discounts cannot be combined with other promotional offers).
 * Carte de Fidélité: For 9 or more purchases of a minimum of €5.00 each, receive a 20% discount on your 10th purchase! (Excluding sales and promotions). Don't hesitate to ask for it when you visit.
 * Events, shows and exhibitions at the Citadelle : special rates or free admission, please enquire.
 * Escape Game (from July 2025) : subscriber rate of €19 regardless of the number of participants (rates up to €27 without subscription).

 * Curse of the Centuries" : Subscriber price €4.20 instead of €5.50 without subscription. Subject to availability.
 * Citadelle admission : If you accompany your friends, they benefit from a reduced rate of €9.50.
 * Refer a Friend Plan : Refer a number of subscribers and extend your subscription (1 referred subscriber = 1 extra month free). By sponsoring a new subscriber (someone who has never subscribed to La Citadelle before), you give them an extra month on their annual subscription, and you yourself get an extra month when you renew your subscription. By sponsoring several new subscribers, you receive several months free when you renew your subscription.
