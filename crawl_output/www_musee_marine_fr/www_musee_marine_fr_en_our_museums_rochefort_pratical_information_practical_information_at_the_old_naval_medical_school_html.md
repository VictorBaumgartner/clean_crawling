# Practical information at the Old Naval Medical School
Visits to the Old Naval Medical School (AEMN), its library and collections are by guided tour only. 
## Access and opening times
### Access
Old Naval Medical School
25, rue amiral Meyer 17300 Rochefort GPS : Hôpital de la Marine Bus : Bus lignes A, B, E, F, G et I, arrêt Denfert-Rochereau ou Les Thermes
Access to the library for consultation of works is by appointment only.
### Opening times
“Essential” guided tour, duration = 45 minutes:
1 september to 22 october : 2 and 3 p.m. daily closed on Tuesdays
23 october to 5 november : 2 and 3 p.m. daily 
6 novembre to 22 december : 2 and 3 p.m. daily closed on Tuesdays
23 december to 7 january : 2 and 3 p.m. daily 
Annual closure from 6 january till 7 february, 1 May and 25 December.
### Accessibility
The Old Naval Medical School building is not accessible to persons with motor disabilities.
### Information and booking
05 46 99 59 57 rochefort@musee-marine.fr
