# Pratical information at the Arsenal
The museum is currently open 
## Access and opening times
### Access
National Maritime Museum Arsenal of Rochefort
1 place de la Gallissonnière 17300 Rochefort GPS : Palais des Congrès Bus : Navette ligne D, arrêt Palais des Congrès
### Opening times
1 september to 22 october : 10 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. and 1:30 p.m. to 6 p.m. closed on Tuesdays.
23 october to 5 november : 10 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. and 1:30 p.m. to 6 p.m. every day 
6 novembre to 22 december : 1:30 p.m. to 6 p.m. closed on Tuesdays
23 december to 7 january : 1:30 p.m. to 6 p.m. every day 
Annual closure from 6 january till 7 february, 1 May and 25 December.
### Accessibility
The museum is not accessible to people with reduced mobility.
