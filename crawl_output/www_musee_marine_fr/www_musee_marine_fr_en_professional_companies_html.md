# Companies
Welcome to the space dedicated to works councils, tourism and image professionals, communities and associations.
This space enables you to access useful information for:
 * Space rental;
 * Film and photo shoots;
 * Requests for iconographic research
 * Group tours available on all our sites: Brest Castle, Paris (Trocadéro), Port Louis Citadel, Hôtel de Cheusses and the Old Naval Medical School in Rochefort, and Porte de l’Arsenal in Toulon.
