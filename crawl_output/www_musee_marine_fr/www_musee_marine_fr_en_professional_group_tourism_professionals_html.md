## Groups
The museum offers a wide range of guided tours and activities at all its sites, some of which are available in English. Audio guides are available in several languages. 
All group visits, whether self-guided or guided, must be booked in advance. The right to speak is included in the cost of the visit.

## Private access
 * zoom_in Enlarge image : Krief-MNMCercleNeptune-66.jpg © Musée national de la Marine / Nicolas Krief 

Take an exclusive tour of the collections at the musée national de la Marine!
The museum is offering all tourism professionals an exceptional range of private tours of its collections and temporary exhibitions. These tours are a unique opportunity to discover the museum and its treasures in exceptional conditions, outside opening hours.
Guided tours or posted guides in front of emblematic works of the collections; discover the many themes of visits proposed and embark on a sublime voyage at sea !
For more information: privatisation(@)musee-marine.fr
