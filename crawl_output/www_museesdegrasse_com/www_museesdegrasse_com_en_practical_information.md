### OPENING HOURS
MIP, MAHP

 * 1 July to 31 August: 10:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m. Gradual closure of MIP rooms begins at 6.30 p.m.
 * Closed on 1 May, first Monday of the month in January, February, March, October, November, December, 25 December and 1 January.

Ticket office closes 60 minutes before the MIP and 30 minutes at the MAHP.
International Perfume Museum Gardens
 * 1 March to 11 November: 09:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.
 * Closed on 1 May, from 12 November to 28 February 2025.

Ticket office closes 30 minutes before the JMIP closing time.
Villa Fragonard
 * School Holidays (area B): 1:00 p.m. to 5:45 p.m.
 * 1 July au 31 August: 1:00 p.m. to 6:45 p.m.
 * Closed on 1 May, 25 December and 1 January.
 * The villa is closed until further notice.

### GUIDED TOURS
All year long, our tour guides propose free guided visits (ninety minutes long). The visits guided are on several sites.
At the MIP:
 * From September to June (outside school holidays): Saturday at 3:00 p.m.
 * School Holidays: Everyday at 11.00 a.m. and 3.00 p.m., except on Sunday.
 * During the Summer Holidays from Monday, July 7 to Saturday, August 30, 2025: olfactory tours of the museum at 11:00 a.m. and 3:00 p.m., every day except Sundays and public holidays. Visit without reservation - please arrive at reception 15 min beforehand.
 * Guided tour price: € 3 per adult + entrance ticket.

MIP Gardens:
 * Saturday at 10.00 a.m.
 * Individual guided tour: € 3/adult + entry ticket.

At the Museum of the Art and History of Provence:
 * Free guided tour without reservation.

### Available for you...
Available for you at the International Perfume Museum:
 * Specific pathway for children starting at age seven (interactive terminals and devices for touching and smelling).
 * Disabled access.
 * Pathway for the sight disabled (in Braille, objects in relief, olfactory pathway).
 * Boutique.

Available for you at the International Perfume Museum Gardens:
 * Disabled access.
 * Boutique.

ACCEPTED MEANS OF PAYMENT Cash, cheques, CB, cultural pass, Côte d'Azur Card.
VISIT REGULATIONS
 * Animals forbidden,
 * Photos with flash and movies forbidden,
 * No smoking and no vaping,
 * Food forbidden,
 * Forbidden to touch showcases and works,
 * Minors under the age of 15 must be accompanied by an adult to enter the Museums (MIP/MAHP/VILLA & JMIP).

For a visit in all serenity… take advantage of your day to explore Grasse, its museums, historic city, etc. And don’t forget the Parking Relais: a 224-space car park,with easy access (near the SNCF railway station), where you can enjoy free parking all day if you have a public transport ticket! Just leave your car at the P+R car park, where you can catch a Sillages bus (5, CENTIFOLIA) — they run every 15 minutes — at the Gare SNCF railway station, to take you to the city-centre of Grasse. Easy, inexpensive and practical!
